In the figure below, triangle ABC is inscribed in a circle with center O. If arc BC = 110°, what is the measure of ∠BAC?
A   35°
B   45°
C   55°
D   110°
✓

+12 pts · streak ×3

Your answer
C
55°✓
Explanation
The Inscribed Angle Theorem states that an inscribed angle equals half the central angle subtending the same arc.

arc BC = 110° → ∠BAC = 110° ÷ 2 = 55°
